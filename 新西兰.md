RotoruaNZ  ·  新西兰罗托鲁瓦旅游局微信小程序
平台解读：  Rotorua是新西兰北岛的温泉与毛利文化重镇，游客体量增长潜力大。这道题强调"内容到转化"的完整链路——从KOL内容到小程序预订的GMV闭环。打通"看到→想去→下单"。
RotoruaNZ  is  the  Economic  Development  Agency  and  Regional  Tourism  Organisation  for  the  Rotorua  district.  It  works  to  grow  the  local  economy  by  attracting  investment,  supporting  businesses,  and  promoting  Rotorua  as  a  destination  to  live,  work,  study,  and  visit.  As  a  Council  Controlled  Organisation,  RotoruaNZ  partners  with  iwi,  government,  and  industry  stakeholders  to  enhance  the  region’s  reputation  and  deliver  sustainable  growth.
RotoruaNZ  是  Rotorua  地区的经济发展机构与区域旅游组织。其核心使命是==吸引投资、扶持本地企业==，并将  Rotorua  打造为适合**居住、工作、求学与旅行**的目的地，以此持续激活本地经济。作为政府控股机构，RotoruaNZ  与毛利部落（iwi）、政府机构及产业伙伴紧密合作，共同提升区域影响力、推动可持续增长。
核心需求
To  drive  bookings  and  product  visibility  on  the  WeChat  Mini  Program,  while  strengthening  Rotorua’s  presence  in  the  China  market  through  aligned  content,  KOL  engagement,  and  seamless  conversion  pathways.
**依托微信小程序拉动预订转化与产品曝**光；同时通过协同内容、KOL  合作以及顺畅的转化链路，**强化  Rotorua  在中国市场的品牌存在感。**
痛点
Key  challenges  include  a  fragmented  conversion  journey,  inconsistent  product  information,  limited  platform  visibility,  and  varying  operator  readiness  for  the  China  market,  all  of  which  impact  overall  sales  performance.
当前面临的主要挑战包括：**转化路径割裂、产品信息不一致、平台曝光度有限，以及本地运营商对中国市场的准备程度参差不齐。上述因素共同制约了整体销售表现**。
方案范围
An  integrated  approach  combining  content-to-conversion  alignment,  product  optimisation,  targeted  traffic  generation,  and  operator  enablement,  supported  by  clear  campaign  execution  and  data-driven  optimisation.
一套整合型方案，将内容与转化路径打通、产品资产统一优化、**精准引流以**及本地运营商赋能有机结合，并以清晰的活动执行与数据驱动的持续优化作为支撑。
生态增长
Drive  traffic  through  WeChat  ecosystem  channels  (Official  Accounts,  Video  Channels,  KOLs,  and  offline  touchpoints),  while  building  retention  through  private  traffic,  content  engagement,  and  seamless  Mini  Program  conversion  to  create  a  closed-loop  growth  model.
通过微信生态的多个触点（公众号、视频号、KOL  合作、线下触点）拉动新流量；同时借助私域运营、内容互动以及小程序的无缝转化实现留存，最终构建闭环增长模型。
技术与工具
Mandatory  integration  of  Weixin  Pay  and  Mini  Program,  supported  by  QR-based  access,  real-time  product  management,  and  robust  tracking  tools  to  enable  seamless  transactions  and  data-driven  optimisation.
**强制要求接入微信支付与微信小程序；配套二维码入口**、实时的产品管理能力以及可靠的数据追踪工具，以支撑顺畅的交易流程与数据驱动的持续优化。
KPI
Success  will  be  measured  by  bookings  and  GMV,  conversion  rates,  traffic  and  engagement  within  the  Mini  Program,  user  growth  and  retention,  campaign  ROI,  and  overall  product  performance  on  the  platform.
成效将从以下维度综合评估：预订量与  GMV、转化率、小程序内的流量与互动表现、用户增长与留存、活动  ROI，以及产品在平台上的整体表现。
交付要求
A  full-stack  partner  providing  Mini  Program  development,  operational  support,  marketing  execution,  and  data-driven  optimisation,  or  modular  services  based  on  project  requirements.
期望提供包含**小程序开发**、运营支持、营销执行与数据驱动的持续优化的完整落地方案。
笔试作品的最基本提交要求是**微信小程序和后端管理系统DEMO**，二者之间不用连调。
